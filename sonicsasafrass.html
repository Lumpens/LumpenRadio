Regina M. Torres / THE SONIC SASSAFRAS


BIO:


Regina M. Torres started collecting music, making mix tapes, and bopping around the house to freeform college radio as a kid in Chicago. Since then, she was a trash can lid banger for an unofficial teenage band, did some voice work for the band Steltch, and was a vocalist/percussionist of the 2-person musical project Drill Scare, which created soundtrack music to accompany cult horror films. Since 2006, she has been a freeform DJ for WZRD. She has co-hosted for The Flabby Hoffman Radio Extravagonzo on Que4 Radio AM 1680. She is also a published writer, visual artist, ESL tutor, animal lover, bike enthusiast, activist, and grad student finishing a MA Linguistics degree at Northeastern Illinois University.
