LUMPEN RADIO IS HERE.

Public Media Institute is a grass roots arts and media non profit organization based in Chicago. PMI’s mission is to create, incubate and sustain innovative cultural programming through the production of socially engaged projects, festivals, spaces, exhibitions, and media. In the past 14 years, PMI has published the art and culture magazines, Lumpen and Proximity, as well as Mash Tun Journal, Materiél magazine and The Bridgeport International. PMI also programs the Co-Prosperity Sphere, an experimental cultural center based in Bridgeport, that hosts exhibitions, talks and events. PMI has been celebrated as creating some of the most innovative arts programming in the midwest, as well as running one of the most popular independent art galleries in Chicago. It’s publications are critically acclaimed, award winning, and part of the city’s media diet. Because of this success we have been awarded a new challenge in the form of a radio station.
